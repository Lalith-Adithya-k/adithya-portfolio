## Design Rationale

Before diving into the manual, here is my approach to this documentation project:

| Aspect | My Approach |
|--------|-------------|
| **Documentation Philosophy** | Task-based minimalism - users do not read manuals; they seek answers to specific tasks |
| **Structure** | Organized by user goals (ordering, tracking, troubleshooting) rather than app features |
| **Writing Style** | Microsoft Style Guide - consistent terminology ("tap" not "click/press/select"), active voice, second person |
| **Visual Strategy** | Screenshots with callouts, flowcharts for decision-based tasks, consistent icon usage |
| **Accessibility** | Clear headings, bullet points for steps, warning notices for critical actions |

## 1. Getting Started

### 1.1 Downloading and Installing the App

| Platform | Steps |
|----------|-------|
| **iOS** | 1. Open the App Store <br> 2. Tap the search tab <br> 3. Type "Swiggy" <br> 4. Tap **Get** next to the Swiggy app <br> 5. Authenticate with Face ID or password |
| **Android** | 1. Open the Google Play Store <br> 2. Tap the search bar <br> 3. Type "Swiggy" <br> 4. Tap **Install** |

> **📸 SCREENSHOT PLACEHOLDER:** App Store search results showing Swiggy app with download button highlighted

---

### 1.2 Creating Your Account

1. Open the Swiggy app
2. Tap **Create Account**
3. Enter your mobile number
4. Enter the OTP sent via SMS
5. Enter your name and email address (optional)

> **Note:** You can also sign up using Google or Facebook by tapping the respective icons on the login screen.

> **📸 SCREENSHOT PLACEHOLDER:** Account creation screen with numbered callouts for each field

---

### 1.3 Setting Up Your Delivery Address

**To add a delivery address:**

1. From the home screen, tap **Deliver to** at the top
2. Tap **Add New Address**
3. Enter your address details:
   - Flat/House number
   - Building name
   - Area/Locality
   - Landmark (optional but recommended)
4. Tap **Save Address**

> **Pro Tip:** Add landmark details like "near the blue water tank" to help delivery partners find you faster.

> **📸 SCREENSHOT PLACEHOLDER:** Address entry screen with annotated fields showing each input area

---

### 1.4 Adding Payment Methods

Swiggy accepts multiple payment methods:

| Payment Method | Setup Steps |
|----------------|-------------|
| **Credit/Debit Card** | Tap **Cards** → **Add New Card** → Enter card details |
| **UPI** | Tap **UPI** → Select your UPI app → Authorize payment |
| **Cash** | No setup required |
| **Swiggy Money** | Tap **Swiggy Money** → Add funds via card/UPI |

### 2.4 Adding Items to Cart

**To add an item:**

1. Tap on any menu item
2. Select quantity using **+** and **-** buttons
3. If applicable, select customization options (spice level, add-ons)
4. Tap **Add to Cart**

> **📸 SCREENSHOT PLACEHOLDER:** Item detail screen with quantity selector, customization options, and Add to Cart button

**To manage your cart:**

- Tap the cart icon at the bottom right
- Adjust quantities by tapping **+** or **-**
- Remove items by swiping left or tapping the trash icon

> **📸 SCREENSHOT PLACEHOLDER:** Cart screen showing items, quantity controls, and total amount

---

### 2.5 Applying Coupons and Offers

1. From the cart screen, tap **Apply Coupon**
2. View available offers:
   - **Restaurant offers** – discounts from the restaurant
   - **Bank offers** – card-specific discounts
   - **Swiggy offers** – platform-wide promotions
3. Tap **Apply** next to your chosen offer

> **Important:** Coupons cannot be combined. The best available discount is applied automatically.

> **📸 SCREENSHOT PLACEHOLDER:** Offers screen showing available coupons with Apply buttons and validity information

### 2.6 Checkout and Payment

1. On the cart screen, verify:
   - Delivery address
   - Payment method
   - Final total
2. Tap **Proceed to Payment**
3. Complete payment based on your selected method
4. Tap **Place Order**

> **After placing your order:** You will see a confirmation screen with an estimated delivery time. This screen includes your order number for reference.

## 3. Managing Your Order

### 3.1 Tracking Your Order in Real-Time

After placing your order, the app shows three stages:

| Stage | What Happens | Visual Indicator |
|-------|--------------|------------------|
| **Preparing** | Restaurant is cooking your order | Orange progress bar |
| **Picked Up** | Delivery partner is en route to restaurant | Blue progress bar |
| **Out for Delivery** | Delivery partner is heading to you | Green progress bar with map view |

> **Tap the map** to see the delivery partner's live location and estimated arrival time.

## 6. Troubleshooting

### 6.1 Payment Failures

| Error Message | Likely Cause | Solution |
|---------------|--------------|----------|
| "Payment declined" | Insufficient balance | Check bank balance or try another method |
| "Transaction timeout" | Network issue | Wait 5 minutes and check order status before retrying |
| "Bank server error" | Temporary bank outage | Use a different payment method |

**If payment was deducted but order not confirmed:**
1. Do **not** retry payment
2. Check order status in **Orders** tab
3. If status shows "Failed," refund will process automatically within 3-5 business days

> **📸 SCREENSHOT PLACEHOLDER:** Payment failure screen with recommended actions and support contact option

---

### 6.2 Order Not Confirmed

**If your order shows "Processing" for more than 5 minutes:**

1. Force close the app and reopen
2. Check **Orders** tab for status update
3. If still unconfirmed, contact support:
   - Profile icon → Help & Support → Order Issues

> **📸 SCREENSHOT PLACEHOLDER:** Order stuck on processing screen with recommended actions

---

### 6.3 App Crashes or Freezes

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| App crashes on open | Update to latest version → Clear app cache → Reinstall |
| App freezes during order | Force close → Clear background apps → Restart app |
| Images not loading | Check internet connection → Toggle Wi-Fi/Data |

**To clear app cache (Android):**
Settings → Apps → Swiggy → Storage → Clear Cache

**To clear app cache (iOS):**
Offload App (Settings → General → iPhone Storage → Swiggy → Offload App) then reinstall
