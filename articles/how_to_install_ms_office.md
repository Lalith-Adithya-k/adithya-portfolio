## 1. Introduction
  
Installing Microsoft Office is essential for anyone looking to enhance productivity and create professional documents, spreadsheets, and presentations. This guide will cover everything you need to know, from purchasing the right version to installing and activating the software on your computer. Whether you’re a first-time user or upgrading to a new version, this document will provide you with clear steps and helpful tips to ensure a smooth installation process.

---

## 2. System Requirements
Before beginning, ensure that you meet the following requirements:
- A compatible operating system (Windows 10, Windows 11).
- A reliable internet connection.
- A Microsoft account (email and password).
- A payment method to complete the purchase.

---

## 3. Sign in into your Microsoft Account
1. Open your web browser and go to [https://www.office.com](https://www.office.com).
2. Click `Sign in` (A) in the Microsoft home page.

![Image 1](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Lalith-Adithya-k/AdithyaTechWorks/main/Sign%20in.png)

3. Enter your Microsoft account email address and click `Next` (A).

![Image 2](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Lalith-Adithya-k/AdithyaTechWorks/main/email.png)

4. Enter your Microsoft account password and click `Sign in` (A).

![Image 3](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Lalith-Adithya-k/AdithyaTechWorks/main/Screenshot%202024-09-30%20171755.png)

5. If you don’t have a Microsoft account, click `Create one!` (A) and perform the on-screen instructions.

![Image 4](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Lalith-Adithya-k/AdithyaTechWorks/main/MS%20office%20illustrations/create%20accnt.png)

- **Note:** After signing in, you will be redirected to your account’s homepage.

## 4. Purchase Microsoft Office or Microsoft 365

### Select the appropriate Office product or subscription plan:
<details>
<summary><strong>Microsoft 365 for home</strong></summary>

1. Go to [Microsoft 365 Plans](https://www.microsoft.com/en-in/microsoft-365/buy/compare-all-microsoft-365-products?icid=MSCOM_QL_M365&culture=en-in&country=in) to view and select the necessary package.
   
   - **Microsoft 365 Family**: $99.99/year (for up to 6 people).
   - **Microsoft 365 Personal**: $69.99/year (for one person).

![Image 5](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Lalith-Adithya-k/AdithyaTechWorks/main/MS%20office%20illustrations/home%20package%20.png)

2. Select `Buy Now` for the selected option.
3. Complete the purchase by providing your payment information.
  - **Note:** Once purchased, you will receive a confirmation email.
  - **Note:** You will be redirected to your Microsoft account dashboard.

</details>
<details>
<summary><strong>Microsoft 365 Business Plans</strong></summary>

1. Go to [Microsoft 365 Business Plans](https://www.microsoft.com/en-in/microsoft-365/business/compare-all-microsoft-365-business-products?icid=MSCOM_QL_M365&culture=en-in&country=in) to view and select the necessary package.

   - **Microsoft 365 Business Basic**: $6.00 per user/month.
   - **Microsoft 365 Business Standard**: $12.50 per user/month.
   - **Microsoft 365 Business Premium**: $22.00 per user/month.
   - **Microsoft 365 Apps for Business**: $8.25 per user/month.

![Image 6](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Lalith-Adithya-k/AdithyaTechWorks/main/MS%20office%20illustrations/Business%20package.png)

2. Choose `Buy Now` for the selected option.
3. Complete the purchase by providing your payment information.
  - **Note:** Once purchased, you will receive a confirmation email.
  - **Note:** You will be redirected to your Microsoft account dashboard.
    

</details>

## 5. Download Office Installation Files
 
1. On the homepage, find and click the `Install apps` (A) button.

![Image 7 ](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Lalith-Adithya-k/AdithyaTechWorks/main/MS%20office%20illustrations/install%20apps.png)

2. Select the necessary version from the drop down.

![Image 8](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Lalith-Adithya-k/AdithyaTechWorks/main/choose%20version.png)

3. Click `Install` (A).

![Image 9](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Lalith-Adithya-k/AdithyaTechWorks/main/MS%20office%20illustrations/wizard%20next.png)

 - **Note:** The setup file will start downloading automatically.
 
4. Wait for the download to finish.

   - **Note:** The file will be saved in your **Downloads** folder.

![Image 10](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Lalith-Adithya-k/AdithyaTechWorks/main/Downloads%20setup.png)

---

## 6. Run Setup
1. Go to your **Downloads** folder and find the `Setup.exe` (A).

![Image 11](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Lalith-Adithya-k/AdithyaTechWorks/main/setup%20icon.png)

2. If prompted by User Account Control, click `Yes` to allow the installer to run.

![Image 12](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Lalith-Adithya-k/AdithyaTechWorks/main/MS%20office%20illustrations/Yes.png)

3. Stay online while the download finishes.

![Image 12](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Lalith-Adithya-k/AdithyaTechWorks/main/MS%20office%20illustrations/wait.png)

4. Once the process is complete, Select `Close` (A).

![Image 13](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Lalith-Adithya-k/AdithyaTechWorks/main/MS%20office%20illustrations/installation.png)

---
## 7. Activate Microsoft Office
1. After installation, open any Microsoft Office application (e.g., Word, Excel, PowerPoint).
2. Sign in with your Microsoft account.

![Image 14](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Lalith-Adithya-k/AdithyaTechWorks/main/MS%20office%20illustrations/activate.png)

3. Use the same Microsoft account associated with your purchase.
---
## 8. Verify Installation
1. Open a Microsoft Office application (e.g., Word).
2. Go to `File` (A).

![Image 15](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Lalith-Adithya-k/AdithyaTechWorks/main/MS%20office%20illustrations/file%20verification.png)

3. Select `Account` (A).

![Image 16](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Lalith-Adithya-k/AdithyaTechWorks/main/MS%20office%20illustrations/account%20verification.png)


3. Check under `Product Information` to confirm that Office is activated.

![Image 17](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Lalith-Adithya-k/AdithyaTechWorks/main/Untitled%20design.png)

## 9. Troubleshoot Common Issues
- **Unable to download the installer**: Check your internet connection and ensure you are signed in to the correct Microsoft account.
- **Installation file not opening**: Verify that your system settings allow installations from external sources, or temporarily disable your antivirus software.
